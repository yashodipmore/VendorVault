MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Master Services Agreement ("MSA") is entered into as of March 1, 2025

PARTIES:
Global Logistics Inc. ("Provider")
123 Shipping Lane, Los Angeles, CA 90001

AND:

Manufacturing Corp ("Client")
456 Industrial Blvd, Detroit, MI 48201

WHEREAS Provider is engaged in providing comprehensive logistics and supply chain
management services, and Client desires to engage Provider for such services.

ARTICLE 1 - SCOPE OF SERVICES
1.1 Provider shall provide comprehensive logistics and supply chain management services
for Client's global distribution network, including:
- Warehousing and inventory management
- Freight forwarding and transportation
- Customs brokerage and compliance
- Last-mile delivery services
- Real-time tracking and analytics

ARTICLE 2 - TERM AND TERMINATION
2.1 Initial Term: 36 months from effective date
2.2 Renewal: Automatic renewal for successive 12-month periods
2.3 Termination for convenience: 90 days written notice
2.4 Termination for cause: 30 days notice with opportunity to cure

ARTICLE 3 - COMPENSATION
3.1 Annual Contract Value: $2,500,000 USD
3.2 Payment Terms: Net 60 days from invoice date
3.3 Volume Discounts: 5% discount for volumes exceeding projections by 20%
3.4 Fuel Surcharge: Variable based on DOE index

ARTICLE 4 - SERVICE LEVELS
4.1 On-time delivery rate: 98.5% minimum
4.2 Inventory accuracy: 99.9% minimum
4.3 Damage rate: Less than 0.1%
4.4 Same-day delivery available for expedited orders

ARTICLE 5 - INSURANCE AND LIABILITY
5.1 Provider maintains $10,000,000 general liability insurance
5.2 Cargo insurance: $500,000 per shipment
5.3 Maximum liability: Annual contract value

ARTICLE 6 - COMPLIANCE
6.1 Provider shall comply with all applicable laws and regulations
6.2 C-TPAT certified facility
6.3 ISO 9001:2015 certified operations

ARTICLE 7 - CONFIDENTIALITY
Both parties shall maintain strict confidentiality of all proprietary information,
trade secrets, and business data exchanged during the term of this Agreement.

ARTICLE 8 - FORCE MAJEURE
Neither party shall be liable for delays caused by circumstances beyond reasonable
control, including natural disasters, war, government action, or pandemic.

ARTICLE 9 - GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.
Disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration in Los Angeles, CA.
